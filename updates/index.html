Updates FAQ
What does NEW mean?

NEW is automatically applied to entries published within the last 14 days. This helps visitors spot the newest release quickly.

I updated and something changed. What should I do?

Read the release notes for your version, then check Support for fixes. If it’s a new issue, report it through your community channel.

Where do I download the latest build?

Use Download hub or go directly to your platform page: iOS, Android, macOS.
